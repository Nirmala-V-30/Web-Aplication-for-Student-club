EVENTS

Online Coding Contest

The online coding contest had taken place from 2nd February to 5th February 2020 on the hackerrank platform.The event had 5 questions with different levels of complexity.All problems are purely based on logical thinking.

Aptitude Contest:

The aptitude contest had taken place on 4th February and it was extended till February 10th.To complete the Aptitude assignment we used a new platform called classmarker.They had conducted the test on different topics like Ages,Boats and Streams ,Compound Interest and Simple Interest.

Paper Presentation Contest

The paper presentation contest held on 14th February 2020.The papers were thoroughly based on new technologies and projects.It had covered several topics like The Game DUGOUT!,COognitive Computing,Bit Coins.

Crossword Puzzle

The crossword puzzle had taken place on 14th February 2020.The puzzle was purely based on technical.It had covered several topics like CN,OS,DBMS etc.

Mock Interview

The mock interview held on 15th February 2020. This mock interview provides students with an oppurtunity to practice the students interview skills in the environment similar to the actual interview.This interview helps the students to learn how to answer difficult questions, develop interview strategies improve the communication skills.

Technical Quiz

The technical quiz contest had taken place on 17th February and it was extended till 19th February 2020.They had conducted the technical quiz based on different topics like Operating system, Database management system and Programming in C.

Turn Coat

The turn coat held on 14 February 2020. The participants were given an on spot technical topics like database management system,Computer Network etc.The time for switch to an opposite party is 2 minutes.
